Truck App (E-Delivery App)

(Part of Integrated Logistics System)

The Truck App is a delivery/transport monitoring app in our logistics system. It allows 3rd party/in-house truckers to complete delivery job scheduled by our admins. It's features includes
  • Package Tracking
  • Trucking Job Execution
  • Electronic Proof of Delivery (ePOD)
    • Record user signature
    • Upload image proof
  • Driver Monitoring and Tracking

The Start of a Truck Job

  1. Anytime a booking that requires truck is scheduled by the admin, a truck job task will be created and will be accessible via a short link provided by us.
  2. Registered 3rd party logistics company can then takeover the transport of goods by using 1 of our custom Android app to complete the truck job.
