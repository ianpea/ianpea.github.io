Customer Portal

(Part of Integrated Logistics System)

In the Customer Portal, any registered customer is able to:
  • Create Booking
    • Inbound/Outbound
    • Freight Services, i.e. truck, air cargo, etc.
  • Monitor Booking Status
    • Processed, completed, anomaly, delayed, etc.
  • View Assigned Warehouse
    • Customers can see a visualised warehouse with their stock info
    • View Items/Products Owned by Them
  • Download POD
Every booking created will be handled by the admin in the admin portal.

Creating a Booking
