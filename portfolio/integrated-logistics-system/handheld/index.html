Handheld Scanner App

(Part of Integrated Logistics System)

The Handheld Scanner App is a customised android applicationd built on top of an industrial scanner (RFID, Barcode, etc). Features include:
  • Check in of goods
  • Check out of goods
  • Relocate Packages/Pallets
  • Stocktaking

Usage of the Custom App

  1. Used to recognize in-house RFID tags and barcode to track and record flow of goods at any entry point.
  2. Allowed for the possibility of logging in with different users, which allowed us to track work/hours.
